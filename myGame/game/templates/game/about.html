About Game

Classic Hand Cricket Game is a number guessing based Cricket Game for all the cricket enthusiasts over there. Many of us have played a game called Hand Cricket in our school days, where two players, one is presumed to be bowling while the other is batting. Like the rock paper scissors game, here both the players signal their guessed number with their hand simultaneously, and when both the numbers match, the batter is said to be dismissed and else the batter would add up the number he put to his/her score. And when eventually the batter is dismissed, the roles are reversed and the other player now has to chase down the total to win the game, if not the first batter would be the winner!.

So this game is made in such a way that, the computer would be the other player and plays optimally, it keeps guessing a random number out of 1 to 6 just like the player playing, giving us the experience of playing the original hand cricket game.
