RULES:

  • Game starts with the toss, whoever wins the toss has to chose either to bat or bowl first.
  • This is a guess based cricket game, when batting or bowling, player has to guess a number between 1 to 6.
  • Computer also guesses a number, if the player's & computer's guess matches, the batting team loses it's wicket.
  • First batting team would bat till they get dismissed and their score is the target.
  • Second batting team would have to chase down the target set to win else the first batting team is declared winner.
  • If the scores are level and second batting team is dismissed, game is declared a Tie.
